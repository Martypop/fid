The Rescue

Here at Marty's Pokemon Rescue our number one goal is to place our pokemon in the safest and happiest homes. Before we place one of our beloved pokemon in your home we go through an intense screening process to make sure your home is the right home. After all adding a pokemon to your family is a lifetime commitment!
